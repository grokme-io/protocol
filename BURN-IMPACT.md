**Already delivered:** 305M+ GROK burned from early capsules.

**Ongoing:** New capsules launch as existing ones fill and seal. Each sealed capsule is followed by the next — a continuous burn pipeline.

### Source 2: GROKME ARENA — Meme Battle Protocol

Every battle deposit has a 5% protocol fee automatically swapped to GROK via Uniswap and burned. This happens regardless of which tokens fight.

**Key insight:** The Arena burns GROK even when GROK itself is not involved in the battle. A PEPE vs SHIB battle burns GROK. A DOGE vs FLOKI battle burns GROK. The Arena is a universal GROK burn machine.

**Additional burn:** After settlement, ALL staked tokens from BOTH sides are burned to 0x...dEaD (these are the community tokens, not GROK — but the 5% fee already converted to GROK and burned at deposit).

## The Antifragility Factor

**Traditional deflation** depends on one mechanism. GROKME has three independent burn sources that compound:

1. **If Arena grows** → more battles → more 5% fees → more GROK burned
2. **If [REDACTED] succeeds** → high-volume daily burns → massive supply reduction
3. **If capsule culture grows** → more capsules → more preservation burns → more GROK burned

**If GROK price drops:**
- Lower price = more GROK burned per dollar of activity
- Supply reduction accelerates
- Scarcity catches up faster

**If GROK price rises:**
- Higher price = less GROK burned per dollar, but higher USD value of each burn
- Existing NFTs and trophies become more valuable
- More attention → more participants → more burns

**The system wins in both directions.** This is structural antifragility.
